The Problem

In the past, brands and retailers have relied on market research and sales data to make decisions about their supply chains. The problem is that in emerging markets, most of this data is delayed or unavailable because it's not collected at the point of sale which leads to 20-40% loss of revenues for companies operating globally in emerging markets.

This means that consumer goods distribution companies are in the dark when it comes to what they should be doing to ensure that goods get to where they need to be at the right time. This can lead to missing out on opportunities for growth and expansion, not meeting customer needs and expectations, and not being able to take advantage of new technologies that could make operations —and therefore bottom lines — run more smoothly.

That's what Libera Foundation is here to change. We harness the power of Web 3.0 to ensure that your company is making informed decisions about everything from product placement and pricing to new product development and merchandise assortment. With our help, you will be able to stay competitive and accelerate your growth by using real-time data for confidently making key business decisions.

Libera token

We're Connecting the Dots Between Consumers, Brands, and Retail.

Libera is an open, distributed ledger that provides a more reliable, efficient and transparent approach to record trade processes on goods and services across any industry. This blockchain infrastructure will allow for the independent reporting of accurate supplier data across the supply chain throughout emerging markets.

Micro-incentives powered by Web 3.0 are a way to drive usage and scalability, but our app is a little different than most others you've seen because it manages inventory and connects small retail stores with consumers to drive sales growth for these retailers.

We'll get retailers on board by giving them tokens for their participation, which they can then use however they want! It's like cash, but better. So yep, the idea is pretty simple: Get the app, earn tokens.

How it works

Decentralized eCommerce with AI

A Customer comes to the store

Shopkeeper is already equiped with Libera POS app which he uses to scan and sell the goods

With the help of blockchain, the app validates and collects every bit of data from the purchase like SCU ID, category, price, quantity, time and location

This data is further distributed to Libera Data Ocean which securely stores it

Using the unique AI and ML algorithms, we process and structures the data to create smart recommendations, optimize inventories and forecast demand

Thanks to Web 3.0 Libera is able to further sell this high value data to FinTechs, MNCs and as an incentive shares the customized projections with store owners to improve their business

About the app

Explore our mobile point of sales Dapp and join us on the mission to transform the modern retail supply chain for billions of humans.

Libera is powerful 3 in 1 mobile application that will fill the largest information gap in Emerging markets supply chains “What consumers are buying” In addition to the technical infrastructure needed to capture and send market data, we have extended the Libera blockchain platform with unique full poS functionalities. We include our smart token that connects all of our platforms together in a single blockchain. Libera will generate revenue from your mobile phone, by posting orders and receipts at the point-of-sale, providing accurate and detailed invoices while keeping records of all financial transactions

Who we serve

Libera targets over a billion consumers in emerging markets, who are largely missing out on the economic benefits of working within global value chains.

According to the World Economic Forum, global GDP could increase by nearly 5% by 2025 through blockchain solutions in sustainable supply chains. Libera is a B2B solution that solves problems that plague large enterprises whose goods travel through these regions—by creating a provenance trail supported by validated data reported directly from the people that form the foundation of every country’s supply chains, companies will be able to better allocate their working capital.
